About

Myopia Elemental Ruling is fantasy battle arena game, where you have to craft your spells befor entering the battle.
You can battle in Arenas or for territory in World.
You can choose from 4 different races and you can customize your looks.
There are achievements and achievemnet based consumables.
The game does not have levels, quests or storyline.
Spells

Spells are crafted by combning different effects. Every spell will have 12 points that you can distribute between different effects and attributes.
Spell effects:
  • Cooldown(default value is 24 seconds.)
  • Range - grants your spell longer range.
  • Area - grants your spells area of affection. Good for multi targeting.
  • Heal - instantly heals you or a friendly target.
  • HOT - healing over time.
  • Damage - instantly damages enemy target.
  • DOT - damage over time.
  • Swift - increases movement speed.
  • Slow - decreases movement speed.
  • Blink - teleport to target location.
  • Grasp - pull and enemy to your location.
  • Affinity - makes your spells stronger.
  • Weakness - makes your enemies spells weaker.
  • Cleanse - removes negative over time effects.
  • Purge - removes positive over time effects from enemy target.
  • Shielding - blocks intant hostile spells.
  • Sealing - block instant friendly splls cast on enemy target.
  • Invisibility - you become invisible.
  • Detect Invisibility - you can see invisible players.
Races

There are four races in the game.
  • Feral
  • Elves
  • Demonic
  • Humans

Spirit Guardian
Spirit guardian is your offline helper. If you go offline your guardian will stay in game and will follow the commands you have given him. Player can merge with their guardians while in game, this way you will become more powerful but while merged the guardian cant follow other commands. Players can lend or rent out their guardian. Also guardians can be merged into a bigger guardian.
